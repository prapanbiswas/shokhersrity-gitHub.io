About Us

At ShokherSrity, we are dedicated to the art of wedding photography. We specialize in capturing the beautiful moments of your special day as you exchange vows and celebrate your love. Our passion lies in documenting these precious memories that you'll cherish forever.

Our professional team, led by Kowsik Saha and Dip Pal, combines technical expertise with artistic vision to create stunning images that tell your unique love story. From candid emotions to perfectly posed portraits, we capture every detail of your wedding celebration.

We pride ourselves on our ability to work seamlessly with couples, their families, and other vendors, ensuring a smooth experience while delivering beautiful, emotional photographs that will be treasured for generations.
